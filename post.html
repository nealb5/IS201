Unlock Your Potential with Personalized Coaching from a former Wide Receiver

Packages

All of our training packages include personalized instruction from a professional wide receiver coach, a focus on skills development, and an emphasis on proper technique and form. We believe in keeping group sizes small to ensure that each player receives individual attention and feedback.




  • Beginner Package

    • This package is designed for players who are new to the position and want to develop a solid foundation of skills. The package includes six 90-minute group sessions, with a maximum group size of 10 players.

    $85 per session

  • Intermediate Package

    • This package is ideal for players who have some experience as a wide receiver and are looking to refine their skills. The package includes eight 90-minute group sessions, with a maximum group size of 8 players.

    $94 per session

  • Advanced Package

    • This package is for experienced players who are looking to take their game to the next level. The package includes ten 90-minute group sessions, with a maximum group size of 6 players.

    $100 per session

  • Custom Package

    • This package is designed for players who have specific goals or areas of focus they want to work on. The package includes a personalized training plan and one-on-one sessions with the coach. The price per session for this package will vary depending on the specific needs of the player.

    $125 per session
