About Us

Welcome to my wide receiver camp! My name is Neal Bartolomei, and I am a former football player with extensive experience as a wide receiver. Throughout my career, I had the privilege of playing for several different high level teams and worked with coaches and former players from the NFL, semi-pro teams, and D1 programs.

Over the years, I have developed a deep understanding of what it takes to succeed as a wide receiver, both on and off the field. Now, I am excited to share my knowledge and expertise with the next generation of players.

At my training camp, we focus on developing the skills and techniques that are essential for success as a wide receiver. Whether you're a beginner just starting out or an experienced player looking to take your game to the next level, we have the resources and expertise to help you achieve your goals.
